What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimisation, and is the process of making a website rank above other websites. Search Engines like Google use algorithms based off the PageRank algorithm, which judges the importance webpages based on links.

Scientific papers are deemed more reliable if they have more citations, and the same applies to webpages. If more sites link to your page, its rank will be higher.
